Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What type of food should I feed my cat?

A: Cats thrive on a high-protein diet, especially from meat. You can find quality dry or wet food that suits their needs.

Q: How often should I take my cat to the vet?

A: It's recommended to take your cat for a check-up at least once a year, or more often if they have specific health concerns.

Q: Is it necessary to groom my cat regularly?

A: Yes, brushing your cat regularly helps remove loose fur and reduce hairballs. Long-haired cats require more frequent grooming.
